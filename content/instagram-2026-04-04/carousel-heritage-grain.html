Heritage vs.
Modern Wheat

Modern wheat was bred for one thing: maximum yield. Heritage varieties like Turkey Red and Bolles have been around 100+ years. Bred for flavor, nutrition, and resilience — not factory efficiency.

Fiddlers Green Farm

Stone-Milled, Not
Roller-Milled

Roller mills strip the bran and germ to extend shelf life. Our grain is stone-milled at a local mill in Randallstown, MD — whole kernel, everything intact. More fiber, more protein, more of the stuff that makes bread actually taste like bread.

Fiddlers Green Farm

Why It
Bakes Different

Heritage flour absorbs water differently and has lower gluten than commercial flour. Your dough will feel different. Your bread will have more flavor and a denser, chewier crumb. Most people never go back.

Fiddlers Green Farm

No Pesticides.
No Fungicides. Ever.

Our fields are USDA certified organic. We manage weeds mechanically and through crop rotation. It’s more work. Way more work. But the soil gets healthier every year instead of more depleted.
