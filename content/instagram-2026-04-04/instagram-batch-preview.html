Heritage vs. Modern Wheat
Modern wheat was bred for one thing: maximum yield. Heritage varieties like Turkey Red and Bolles have been around 100+ years. Bred for flavor, nutrition, and resilience — not factory efficiency.
Stone-Milled, Not Roller-Milled
Roller mills strip the bran and germ to extend shelf life. Our stone mill crushes the whole kernel and keeps everything intact. More fiber, more protein, more of the stuff that makes bread actually taste like bread.
Why It Bakes Different
Heritage flour absorbs water differently and has lower gluten than commercial flour. Your dough will feel different. Your bread will have more flavor and a denser, chewier crumb. Most people never go back.
No Pesticides. No Fungicides. Ever.
Our fields are USDA certified organic. We manage weeds mechanically and through crop rotation. It's more work. Way more work. But the soil gets healthier every year instead of more depleted.
78 Years on the Same Land
Fiddlers Green Farm has been in operation since 1946. Ben is the current generation. We grow it, clean it, and work with a local stone mill in Randallstown, MD to turn it into flour. From our fields to your kitchen — as few steps as possible.
