Executive Summary

Fiddlers Green Farm scores 51/100 on our comprehensive marketing audit, placing it in the "Below Average" tier with significant gaps that need addressing before the business can scale. This is not a reflection of product quality — the farm has genuinely strong foundations — but rather of marketing execution that has not kept pace with what the products deserve.

The biggest strength is brand authenticity. A multi-generational family farm (since 1946), USDA organic certification, regenerative farming practices, and a transparent team with real wellness credentials create a trust narrative that most CBD brands spend millions trying to fabricate. The 5.0-star rating across Google (6 reviews) and product reviews (4.76-5.0 stars) confirms customers love what they're buying.

The biggest gap is the complete absence of a growth engine. There is no functioning blog (2 test posts from 2021), no content marketing, no referral program, no loyalty program, limited payment options (no PayPal, no Apple Pay, no BNPL), and the site displays a months-old holiday shipping banner that signals abandonment. The business is effectively invisible to new customers beyond Instagram and word-of-mouth.

The top 3 actions that would move the needle most: (1) Fix critical trust-eroding signals (remove stale banner, link lab COAs on product pages, fix robots.txt), (2) Add modern payment options and a first-order discount popup to stop losing ready-to-buy visitors, and (3) Launch a content marketing engine leveraging the farm's rich story to drive organic discovery. Implementing all recommendations could generate an estimated $3,000–$12,000/month in additional revenue within 6 months.

Competitive Positioning Analysis (58/100)

Fiddlers Green occupies a genuinely unique position as a multi-generational organic farm producing both CBD and heritage flour. This "whole farm" identity is distinctive — neither national DTC brands (Cornbread Hemp, Head & Heal) nor local competitors (Fingerboard Farm) combine CBD with artisan grain under one regenerative agriculture story.

However, the DTC CBD market has professionalized. Cornbread Hemp has 26K+ Instagram followers, editorial placements, and robust subscription programs. Head & Heal commands premium pricing ($120 for 2400mg oil). Even Fingerboard Farm in Frederick, MD has diversified into CBN/CBG and operates physical retail. Fiddlers Green's pricing ($10-$80) is competitive but the advantage goes uncommunicated without stronger marketing.
