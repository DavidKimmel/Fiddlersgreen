SEO AUDIT REPORT: FiddlersGreen.io

Fiddlers Green Farm | CBD Products & Organic Flour | Taneytown, MD
Platform: WordPress / WooCommerce with AIOSEO Plugin
Audit Date: March 18, 2026

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Fiddlers Green Farm is a family-owned organic hemp farm in Taneytown, Maryland selling CBD products (topicals, extracts, edibles, pet products) and stone-milled heirloom wheat flour via a WooCommerce store. The site has strong E-E-A-T fundamentals — multi-generational family farm story, named team members with credentials, organic certification, and third-party lab testing. However, significant technical SEO issues, missing metadata across key pages, an abandoned blog, a stale holiday banner, and thin category page content are limiting organic search visibility. With 33+ products and rich educational content already on the site, there is substantial untapped potential to capture CBD, hemp, and organic flour search traffic.
